About MLB Tweets

What is MLB Tweets?

MLB Tweets is a collection of Twitter lists for all 30 Major League Baseball teams.

Which Twitter accounts are included in the lists?

Lists feature verified Twitter handles for all players on a given team's active 40-man roster.

How often are Twitter lists updated?

Lists are updated automatically at 2:00 AM every Monday.
